About Us

R S Cookware Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2010 by Mr. Naresh Chaudhary. He had a vision of ‘cooking with difference’. He later realized that this vision would take the best technology to be utilized in order to make a high-tech product. Hence, the brand BESTECH was launched under this company. Bestech manufactures quality pressure cookers and cookware which is tested under the supervision of our highly talented team. Bestech has brought together one of the most comprehensive ranges of quality cookware.

We have a number of satisfied customers in India as well abroad. Our quality and commitment in the past many years have played an important role in this accomplishment

Our attempt to transform the best ideas into reality forms the basis of our world. Our quest for perfection, and our indomitable spirit to achieve goals against all odds dominates our thought process.

By combining the advanced engineering technology with a smart sense of styling, Bestech has produced an expanding range of products known for their quality and durability. Our cookware website will help you select the cookware that's right for your individual needs.

Quality


It is a well-known fact that a pressure cooker bears a higher pressure than that of ambient pressure, thus necessary precautions are required in the design, manufacturing and use of the domestic pressure cooker to safeguard against accidents in the kitchen. Bestech has adopted the stringent quality control according to (ISI) specified norms and has a well-equipped separate lab for testing purposes. Every single product from our unit passes through a stringent quality control process to ensure error free quality delivery.


Following are our quality objectives:

  • Consistently meeting or exceeding our customer’s expectations for product quality & performance.
  • Timely delivery of products & services to meet our customer’s requirement.
  • Continuous improvement of our processes and system.
  • Ensuring our personnel are properly trained.

We are an ISO-9001:2008 certified company.
