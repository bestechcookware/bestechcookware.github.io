Naresh Chaudhary

Director

Mr. Naresh Chaudhary started his business with a small unit in Jalandhar, Punjab in 1989. He became an entrepreneur at an early age of 16. With his hard work, clear vision and deep understanding of the business, he is today the owner of more than 6 companies in diverse sectors such as software, construction, cookware manufacturing and food processing. He is also well known in the industry for his helping hand to many entities and active social work.
